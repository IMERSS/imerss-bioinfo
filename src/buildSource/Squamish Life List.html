Átl’ka7tsem/Howe Sound UNESCO Biosphere Reserve Species Dataset

Last updated November 2022.

A preliminary visualisation of the species list for the Átl’ka7tsem/Howe Sound UNESCO Biosphere Reserve, compiled by the Squamish Environment Society under the direction of Judith Holm.

Note: this visualization is based on a working species list and may contain some errors and redundancies. As a work in progress it is continually subject to review with assistance from various taxonomic authorities. Please contact the project curator if you identify any errors.
